# Tip: It’s more than exercise

**For non-clinical participants:** Cardiac rehab is whole-person care. Solutions that only track steps miss most of what the program actually does.

---

## What a session can look like

People often think of cardiac rehab as a gym program, but it covers much more. A typical session might include:

- A **supervised workout** on a treadmill or bike  
- A short **class** on reading nutrition labels  
- A **conversation** about managing stress at home  
- A **check-in** on how the patient is feeling emotionally  

## What the team works on over time

Across the program, the team helps patients:

- Lower **blood pressure**  
- Manage **cholesterol**  
- **Quit smoking**  
- **Lose weight** (when appropriate)  
- **Stick with medications**  

## Takeaway for your solution

Any prototype you build should reflect this **breadth**. Cardiac rehab treats the **whole person**, not just the heart—so education, mood, adherence, and lifestyle belong in the same conversation as exercise.
